Varanasi Weather and Best Time to Visit


Varanasi experiences a hot semi-arid climate. The summer months, from April to June, can be extremely hot, with temperatures soaring above 40 degrees Celsius (104 degrees Fahrenheit). The monsoon season, from July to September, brings heavy rainfall. The ideal time to visit Varanasi is during the winter months, from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and suitable for exploring the city and its surroundings.

Festivals of Varanasi


Varanasi is synonymous with vibrant festivals that celebrate the city's rich cultural heritage. Some of the most popular festivals include:
Diwali: Experience the grandeur of Diwali, the festival of lights, as the ghats and streets of Varanasi illuminate with countless diyas (oil lamps) and fireworks.
Mahashivaratri: Join the millions of devotees who flock to Varanasi to celebrate Mahashivaratri, a significant Hindu festival dedicated to Lord Shiva.
Dev Deepawali: Witness the magnificence of Dev Deepawali, a festival that occurs on the day of Kartik Purnima, as the ghats are adorned with thousands of lamps to welcome the gods and goddesses.
