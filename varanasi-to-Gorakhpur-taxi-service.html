The Best Way to Reach and Explore This Enchanting City Gorakhpur


Gorakhpur, a city in the northeastern part of Uttar Pradesh, India, holds immense historical and cultural significance. Renowned for its rich heritage, religious sites, and natural beauty, Gorakhpur attracts tourists from all corners of the globe. If you're planning a visit to this captivating city, Big Cab for Gorakhpur is your trusted companion, offering convenient and reliable transportation services. In this article, we will explore how to reach Gorakhpur, why you should visit, and the top things to see and do in this remarkable destination.

How to Reach Gorakhpur:
Gorakhpur is well-connected through various modes of transportation, ensuring easy accessibility for travelers. Here are the most convenient ways to reach Gorakhpur:

By Air: Gorakhpur Airport, also known as Mahayogi Gorakhnath Airport, is situated approximately 8 kilometers from the city center. Several domestic airlines operate regular flights to Gorakhpur from major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, and Lucknow. From the airport, you can avail yourself of Big Cab for Gorakhpur services to reach your desired destination in the city.

By Train: Gorakhpur Junction is one of the busiest railway stations in North India, well-connected to various parts of the country. It serves as a major junction for trains traveling to major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, and Varanasi. With a vast network of railways, Gorakhpur is easily accessible from any corner of India.

By Road: Gorakhpur enjoys excellent road connectivity with neighboring cities and states. National Highway 27 passes through the city, linking it to major destinations like Varanasi, Lucknow, and Patna. You can opt for Big Cab for Gorakhpur from nearby cities or hire a taxi to enjoy a comfortable journey.


Why Visit Gorakhpur:


Gorakhpur offers a delightful blend of cultural heritage, religious significance, and natural beauty. Here are a few reasons why you should visit Gorakhpur:

Gorakhnath Temple: The Gorakhnath Temple, dedicated to Guru Gorakhnath, is a prominent religious site in Gorakhpur. This ancient temple attracts devotees seeking blessings and spiritual solace.
Kushmi Forest: Located on the outskirts of Gorakhpur, Kushmi Forest is a tranquil retreat for nature lovers. Explore the lush greenery, take a peaceful walk, or enjoy a picnic amidst the serene surroundings.
Gorakhpur Railway Station: One of the oldest and architecturally stunning railway stations in India, Gorakhpur Junction is a must-visit for history and railway enthusiasts. Admire the grandeur of its intricate design and learn about its historical significance.
Ramgarh Tal: Situated around 30 kilometers from Gorakhpur, Ramgarh Tal is a picturesque lake offering boating facilities. Spend a leisurely afternoon by the serene waters, surrounded by scenic landscapes.
Things to See and Do in Gorakhpur: Gorakhpur has a plethora of attractions and activities to offer. Here are some must-see places and things to do in the city:
Gorakhpur Museum: Explore the rich cultural heritage of Gorakhpur at the Gorakhpur Museum. It houses a vast collection of artifacts, sculptures, ancient coins, and historical relics.
Arogya Mandir: Visit Arogya Mandir, a unique healthcare center in Gorakhpur that combines ancient Ayurvedic practices with modern medical treatments. Experience rejuvenation and wellness amidst a serene environment.
Railway Museum: Delve into the fascinating world of railways at the Railway Museum in Gorakhpur. Discover vintage locomotives, antique railway artifacts, and exhibits showcasing the evolution of Indian railways.
Vishnu Mandir: Pay homage to Lord Vishnu at the Vishnu Mandir, a famous temple in Gorakhpur known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
